Chapter One
I
THE MISSION
🚀
Captain Cody surveys the stars.
The crew awaits his command.

The mission: unknown.
The resolve: absolute.
No one questioned the Captain's authority.
Not after they saw the teeth.
Chief Scientist Bob ran the calculations.
He was rarely wrong.
Except about parking.
Chapter Two
II
FIRST CONTACT
📡
Leora detected a signal.
Not noise.
Music.
✦ ✧ ✦
The Glimmerfins appeared—
crystalline beings made of light.

They shimmered like fractured starlight,
singing in frequencies no human ear could hold.
💎
They were beautiful.

And terrified.
🎵
Leora translated their song.

"Something is hunting us,"
they sang.

"And it is very, very close."
Chapter Three
III
THE VOID HUNTERS
⚫
The screens went dark.
One by one.

Something was coming.
The Captain did not panic.
He had survived worse.
He had survived baths.
💤
Engineering reported
total power failure.

Vance was already asleep
at his station.
📻
Sue opened a diplomatic channel.

"Wait."

"I think they're just... lost."
Chapter Four
IV
THE THERAPY SESSION IN SPACE
🖖
Sue stepped forward.

"Tell me about your home."
⚫ · ⚫
They had been wandering
so long...

...they'd forgotten
what they were looking for.
Captain Cody rose.
This was the moment.
He had been waiting for this his entire career.
🐾
He barked once.

Soft.

Like he was saying:
"I see you."
Chapter Five
V
HOME
✦✧✦✧✦

harmonic frequency—

"So you can always
find your way back."
Chief Scientist Bob held the Captain close.
Mission complete.
Captain's Log, final entry:
"Good boy."
